Mr. Rabbit's Guide to Sneaky Rhetoric

A guide to the subtle ways language influences our thinking. How the sentences we construct and consume in our news, in our politics, and in our everyday lives impact the way we perceive the world around us. Examples include language that prevaricates, language that conceals with vagueness, and language that feigns objectivity with apparent neutrality.
