Many of my critics fault me for not engaging more directly with the academic literature. My approach is to generally make an end run around many of the views and conceptual distinctions that make academic discussions of human values so inaccessible. While this is guaranteed to annoy a few people, the professional philosophers I’ve consulted seem to understand and support what I am doing.4

The professional philosophers are never named, and one is left wondering whether they do or don’t support the author’s approach. Suppose no supporters can be found: That seem gives the author room to claim an honest misunderstanding.
