LOADED LANGUAGE — I’m thrilled that the project I shared with you all late last year will be out in print this October. It will look absolutely stunning, with 13+ original illustrations and a gorgeous cover by the always outstanding Alejandro Giraldo.

It will have seven chapters, touching on the different, and often subtle, ways in which headlines and various other forms of language influence our thinking.

The book has a new title, you might have noticed. It couldn’t be in better hands, quite honestly. I’ll be sharing updates and photos on Instagram and Twitter as we get closer to release day.

—A.A., June 22, 2021
